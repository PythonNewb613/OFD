At Orleans Family Dentistry, we believe in giving back to the community that has been so good to us. The OFD team is involved with many charitable initiatives and organizations across this great city. Here are a few examples:

Orleans Family Dentistry proudly displaying our banner for Santa's Parade of Lights!

Adopt a Roadway

Adopt-a-Park or Road is a city-wide program that encourages community involvement in the care and maintenance of parks and roadways. It is a partnership between volunteers and the City. OFD is showing support by adopting a section of St.Joseph Blvd from Prestone Dr to Belcourt Blvd.

Trick or Treat With the Mayor!

The Mayor invites you to an evening of safe Halloween fun in support of the Ottawa Food Bank’s Baby Basics Program

Children at Risk

The OFD team is a proud partner of the Children at Risk Charity, which provides services to the families of children dagnosed with Autism.

Orleans Lions Club

The OFD team is a proud sponsor of the Orleans Lions Club Walkathon.

Did you know?

Our Orleans dental clinic sits on historic land! Georges Gauthier - one of Orlean's earliest settlers - built his well-known leather crafts shop on this very location. We are proud to display one of 32 Historic Plaques, which was presented to us by the Société Franco-Ontarienne de Patrimoine et de l'Histoire d'Orléans (SFOPHO) and the Heart of Orleans BIA, in front of our building.
