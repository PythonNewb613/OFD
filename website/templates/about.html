Orleans Family Dentistry's goal is to provide personalized, high-quality care on an as-needed or preventative basis. We have created a practice that we believe in and choose for our own family members.

We work together to serve your entire family for all of your dental needs in all stages of life. Our team believes in providing comprehensive oral health care services to our patients in a friendly, relaxed atmosphere. We also believe in educating our patients as we work with them to achieve their oral health goals.

Fluent in english & french, Dr. Bhatia and Dr. Cadieux are certified by the Royal College of Dental Surgeons of Ontario and have been each practicing for over 15 years. They are both proud members of the Ottawa Dental Society, the Ontario Dental Association and the International Association for Orthodontics.

Continuing Education is also a very high priority for the dentists, as they are continually taking courses and attending seminars on the latest dental techiniques and procedures so that they can provide their patients with the highest standards of professional care.
